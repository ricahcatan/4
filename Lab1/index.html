Vision

A Research University advancing technology and innovation for sustainable development

Mission

NEMSU shall provide competency-based higher education through transformative instruction, relevant research, sustainable extension and production responsive to local, regional and global trends

Quality Policy

NEMSU is committed to producing highly motivated, globally competitive, and morally upright human resources. This is achieved by delivering transformative and quality higher education that conforms to international standards, driven by excellent instruction, relevant research, sustainable extension, and production services. The university actively endeavors for continual improvement of its quality management system in compliance with regulatory requirements.
